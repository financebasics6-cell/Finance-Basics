About Finance Basics Agent

Finance Basics Agent is a local desktop application that runs on your computer. It helps you create and upload financial education videos to your YouTube channel automatically.

What This App Does

  • Generates video scripts using AI (Gemini)
  • Creates voiceover audio from the script
  • Fetches stock images to accompany the content
  • Assembles the final video
  • Uploads the video to your YouTube channel

All processing runs locally on your computer. We do not host any servers or store your data.

Why We Request Access to Your Data

This app requests access to your YouTube account for one purpose only:

  • To upload videos to your YouTube channel on your behalf. When you authorize the app, you grant it permission to upload videos (title, description, and video file) to the channel associated with your Google account.

We do not access, read, or modify any other data in your Google or YouTube account. The authorization is used solely for the upload process.
