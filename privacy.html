Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Data Collection

Finance Basics Agent runs locally on your computer. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers.

Google/YouTube

When you use this app to upload videos, you authenticate with Google. Only the data necessary for YouTube uploads (video file, title, description) is sent to Google's servers. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party APIs

The app uses Gemini (script generation), Pexels (stock images), and YouTube APIs. Data sent to these services is limited to what's required for the app to function. Review each service's privacy policy for details.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project repository.
